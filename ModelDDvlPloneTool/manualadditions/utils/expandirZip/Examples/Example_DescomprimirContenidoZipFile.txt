Exercising the utility:

Copy one of the souce archived components to the Products folder.

i.e. :

In windows, copy ModelDDvlPlone_NoRoot.zip to 
D:\dvpt\Plone251\Data\Products

Or in tweb logged as zadmin upload ModelDDvlPlone_NoRoot.zip to 
https://gvsig.org/tweb/scripts/listado-de-prueba?path=/var/lib/zope2.9/instance/testing-plone-site/Products


Invoke the script, passing th archived component as argument:

In my local plone with id "bpd":
http://localhost/bpd/Descomprimir_zip_frontEndScript?Path=D:\dvpt\Plone251\Data\Products&ZipFileName=ModelDDvlPlone_NoRoot.zip&IsWindows=Yes&RootDir=ModelDDvlPlone

In tweb:
https://gvsig.org/tweb/Descomprimir_zip_frontEndScript?Path=/var/lib/zope2.9/instance/testing-plone-site/Products&ZipFileName=ModelDDvlPlone_NoRoot.zip&RootDir=ModelDDvlPlone
